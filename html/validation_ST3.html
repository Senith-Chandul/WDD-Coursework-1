Reflection on Validation

During the validation process, I learned the importance of adhering to web standards to ensure compatibility across different browsers and devices. While most pages passed validation without errors, I encountered warnings related to optional attributes. These warnings were addressed by ensuring that all attributes were necessary and correctly implemented. Overall, the validation process helped me improve the quality and accessibility of the website.
